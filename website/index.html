The Path Forward

Understanding Veo's current limitations, ethical guardrails, and future trajectory is crucial for strategic planning and responsible creation.

Current Hurdles

  • 8-second clip length ceiling
  • Inconsistent multi-shot continuity
  • Poor rendering of text/logos
  • Bias towards "cinematic" shots

Ethical Guardrails

  • SynthID invisible watermarking
  • Content filtering for harmful prompts
  • Blocking generation of public figures
  • Adherence to FTC/IAB regulations

Future Trajectory

  • Extending maximum video length
  • Mastering character/style consistency
  • Moving towards real-time generation
  • Deeper integration into Google ecosystem
